Core Features

Everything your OPD needs

Built specifically for hospital outpatient departments — every feature maps directly to your daily workflow.

🏥

Patient Registration

Register patients quickly with full details — name, age, gender, contact, and department. Auto-generates a unique patient ID.

🎫

Token Queue Management

FIFO-based token system assigns each patient a queue number. Displays estimated wait time and current queue status in real time.

👨‍⚕️

Doctor Scheduling

Admins can add, update, and track doctor availability. Patients are automatically assigned to available doctors by department.

📊

Admin Dashboard

Real-time monitoring of patient flow, queue stats, doctor status, and department load — all in a single control panel.

📋

Reports & Analytics

Generate daily and historical reports on patient volume, wait times, and doctor consultations for hospital management.

🔐

Role-Based Access

Separate portals for Admins, Receptionists, and Doctors — each with permissions tailored to their responsibilities.

Workflow

How CareFlow works

A simple, four-step patient journey from arrival to consultation.

1

Patient Arrives

Receptionist registers the patient and captures all required information in the system.

2

Token Generated

System assigns the next available token number and places patient in the FIFO queue.

3

Doctor Assigned

Patient is matched to an available doctor based on department and current schedule.

4

Consultation Done

Doctor updates consultation status, queue advances, and admin dashboard reflects changes.
